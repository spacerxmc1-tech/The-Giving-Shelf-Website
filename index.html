Our Mission

Books deserve a second life. People deserve a story.

The Giving Shelf exists to give books a second life — by collecting used books from the community and placing them into the hands of people in homeless shelters. We believe books provide comfort, education, and hope, and that everyone deserves access to reading materials regardless of their circumstances.

📖

Why books matter

A book is a quiet companion. It offers escape, learning, and the dignity of choosing what you read. For someone navigating hardship, that small autonomy can mean everything.

🧺

How we collect

We partner mainly with community members, plus teachers and libraries clearing shelves. Gently used books of every genre — fiction, non-fiction, kids' titles, poetry — all welcome.

🚚

How we distribute

Donations are sorted, then delivered directly to shelters and outreach organizations across Detroit — including Covenant House Michigan, Cass Community Social Services, and Detroit Rescue Mission Ministries.

🌱

Youth-led, community-fueled

Run by young volunteers who believe small acts compound into real change. Every drop-off, every shelf, every reader — it all adds up.

"A book in the right hands can change a day — or a life."
